Two obsessions. One kitchen.

We started Crunch & Brew because we believed Nairobi deserved fried chicken that actually crackles, and coffee pulled by someone who gives a damn. Five years later, we're still hand-brining every bird and dialing in every espresso shot.

No shortcuts. No frozen chicken. No pre-ground beans. Just two things done obsessively well, served in a space that feels like yours.
